Understanding Ovulation Tracking

Clara estimates your ovulation period based on your cycle history. Ovulation typically occurs about 14 days before your next period. The fertile window spans approximately 5-6 days around ovulation.

Important: These are estimates based on typical patterns. For precise ovulation detection, consider methods like basal body temperature tracking or ovulation predictor kits.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the visual calendar work?

The calendar displays your period data with color-coded indicators directly on each date. Flow intensity is shown with different shades: pink for spotting/light, red for medium/heavy. Your ovulation window appears as a purple gradient overlay when the toggle is enabled.

How is ovulation calculated?

Clara estimates your ovulation period based on your average cycle length. Ovulation typically occurs about 14 days before your next period. The fertile window (shown in purple) spans approximately 5 days around the estimated ovulation day. These are estimates - for precise ovulation tracking, consult your healthcare provider about temperature tracking or ovulation predictor kits.

Can I turn off the ovulation display?

Yes! Use the "Show Ovulation Period" toggle at the top of the calendar to show or hide the ovulation window display.

How do I change the language?

Tap the flag icon below the logo on the main screen and select your preferred language from English, German, Spanish, French, Japanese, or Korean.

How do I restore my premium purchase?

Tap "Unlock Full History" then "Restore Purchase". Make sure you're using the same Apple ID you originally purchased with. All purchases are managed through Apple's secure system.

Is my data private?

Yes! All your tracking data is stored locally on your device only. Clara never uploads your data to servers or shares it with third parties. Your health information stays completely private.

How do I export a PDF report?

Tap "Export PDF Report" on the main screen, select your desired date range for the report, and tap "Export". You can then share or save the PDF for your healthcare appointments.

What does the free version show?

The free version displays the last 2 months of your tracking data. All your data continues to be stored safely on your device - you need Premium to view data older than 2 months.

What are pattern views?

Clara analyzes your logged cycles to identify patterns like cycle length regularity, flow duration, and other trends. These views provide context to help you have informed discussions with your healthcare provider.

How are estimates calculated?

Estimates are calculated based on your logged cycle history. They use your personal data, not generic averages. Remember that cycle variations are normal and all estimates may not be accurate.

Can I delete my data?

Yes, all data is deleted when you delete the app. You have complete control over your information at all times.
