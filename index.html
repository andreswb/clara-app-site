Clara — Your Trusted Period Companion

Track your cycle with privacy and simplicity. Clara is a personal tool for logging your body's natural rhythms, understanding patterns, and preparing for conversations with your healthcare provider.

Features

📅 Visual Calendar

Color-coded flow intensity on every date. Spotting, light, medium, and heavy flows each shown in distinct shades.

💜 Ovulation Window

Estimated fertile window displayed as a purple gradient directly on the calendar, based on your personal cycle data.

💗 Symptom Tracking

Log symptoms throughout your entire cycle — not just during your period. Track cramps, mood, fatigue, bloating, and 12 more.

📊 Pattern Analysis

Detect short, long, or irregular cycles. Identify flow duration changes worth discussing with your doctor.

🔥 Symptom Heatmap

Premium: visualize when each symptom tends to occur across your four cycle phases with color-coded heatmaps.

📄 PDF Export

Generate detailed reports for medical appointments, covering cycles, flow intensity, symptoms, and detected patterns.

🔒 Privacy First

All data stays on your device. No accounts, no cloud sync, no third-party sharing — ever.

🌍 6 Languages

Available in English, German, Spanish, French, Japanese, and Korean. Automatically matches your device language.

Understanding Your Cycle Phases

Clara's help section explains the four phases of the menstrual cycle so you can better understand your symptom patterns.

🩸 Menstrual Phase
Days 1–5

Period bleeding. Common symptoms: cramps, back pain, headache, fatigue.

🌿 Follicular Phase
Days 6–13

Post-period recovery. Generally fewer symptoms, increased energy.

💜 Ovulation Phase

Egg release. Symptoms: breast tenderness, mild cramps, bloating.

🌙 Luteal Phase (PMS)

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the visual calendar work?

The calendar displays your logged period data with color-coded indicators on each date. Flow intensity is shown in distinct shades: light pink for spotting, pink for light, red for medium, and deep red for heavy. The ovulation window appears as a purple gradient when enabled.

How does symptom tracking work?

Tap any calendar date to log symptoms alongside your flow intensity. You can track 16 symptoms including cramps, headache, bloating, mood swings, fatigue, anxiety, and more. Symptoms can be logged during any phase of your cycle — not just during your period. Premium users can view symptom heatmaps showing when each symptom tends to occur across the four cycle phases.

What are the four cycle phases?

Clara's help section explains the menstrual (days 1–5), follicular (days 6–13), ovulation (days 14–16), and luteal/PMS (days 17–28) phases. Understanding these phases helps you recognise patterns in your symptom data.

How are cycle estimates calculated?

Estimates are based on your logged cycle history. Ovulation is estimated as 14 days before your expected next period. The fertile window spans approximately 5–6 days around ovulation. These are estimates based on your personal patterns and may vary.

How do I change the app language?

Go to the Settings menu inside the app and select your preferred language. Clara supports English, German, Spanish, French, Japanese, and Korean. On first launch it automatically matches your device language.

How do I restore my premium purchase?

Tap "Unlock Full History" then "Restore Purchase". Use the same Apple ID as your original purchase. Purchases are managed through Apple's system and work across all your devices.

Does premium work offline?

Yes. Once unlocked, premium status is stored securely on your device via the system keychain and works fully offline and in airplane mode.

What does the free version show?

The free version displays the last 2 months of logged data. Your full data history continues to be stored safely on your device — premium simply unlocks access to data beyond 2 months, along with the symptom heatmap and complete PDF reports.

Is my data private?

Yes. All data is stored locally on your device only. Clara never uploads data to servers or shares it with any third party.

Der Kalender zeigt Ihre protokollierten Periodendaten mit farbcodierten Indikatoren auf jedem Datum. Tippen Sie auf ein Kalenderdatum, um Symptome zusammen mit der Blutungsstärke zu erfassen. Sie können 16 Symptome verfolgen, darunter Krämpfe, Kopfschmerzen, Blähungen, Stimmungsschwankungen und mehr. Premium-Nutzer können Symptom-Heatmaps anzeigen.

Was sind die vier Zyklusphasen?

Claras Hilfebereich erklärt die Menstruationsphase (Tage 1–5), Follikelphase (6–13), Ovulationsphase (14–16) und Lutealphase/PMS (17–28). Das Verständnis dieser Phasen hilft Ihnen, Symptommuster besser zu erkennen.

Wie werden Schätzungen berechnet?

Schätzungen basieren auf Ihrer protokollierten Zyklushistorie. Der Eisprung wird als 14 Tage vor der erwarteten nächsten Periode geschätzt. Das fruchtbare Fenster umfasst etwa 5–6 Tage. Diese Schätzungen können variieren.

Gehen Sie im Einstellungsmenü der App zu Spracheinstellungen. Clara unterstützt Deutsch, Englisch, Spanisch, Französisch, Japanisch und Koreanisch. Beim ersten Start wird die Gerätesprache automatisch erkannt.

Ja. Der Premium-Status wird sicher auf Ihrem Gerät gespeichert und funktioniert vollständig offline und im Flugzeugmodus.

Die kostenlose Version zeigt die letzten 2 Monate protokollierter Daten. Alle Daten werden weiterhin sicher auf Ihrem Gerät gespeichert. Premium schaltet den Zugriff auf Daten über 2 Monate hinaus sowie Symptom-Heatmap und vollständige PDF-Berichte frei.

자주 묻는 질문

시각적 캘린더는 어떻게 작동하나요?

캘린더는 각 날짜에 색상으로 코딩된 지표로 생리 데이터를 표시합니다. 생리량은 소량 출혈, 적음, 보통, 많음으로 다른 색상으로 표시됩니다. 배란 기간은 활성화 시 보라색 그라데이션으로 표시됩니다.

증상 기록은 어떻게 작동하나요?

캘린더의 날짜를 탭하여 생리량과 함께 증상을 기록할 수 있습니다. 복통, 두통, 부종, 기분 변화, 피로 등 16가지 증상을 추적할 수 있습니다. 프리미엄 사용자는 증상 히트맵을 볼 수 있습니다.

프리미엄 구매를 복원하려면?

"전체 기록 잠금 해제"를 탭한 후 "구매 복원"을 선택하세요. 원래 구매에 사용한 Apple ID를 사용하세요.

프리미엄은 오프라인에서 작동하나요?

네. 한 번 잠금 해제되면 프리미엄 상태는 기기에 안전하게 저장되어 완전히 오프라인 및 비행기 모드에서도 작동합니다.

내 데이터는 비공개인가요?

네. 모든 데이터는 기기에만 로컬로 저장됩니다. Clara는 데이터를 서버에 업로드하거나 제3자와 공유하지 않습니다.
